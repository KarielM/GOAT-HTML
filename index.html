GOAT: Kaoru Ochiai

    Education:

  • B.A. - Aoyama Gakuin University
  • M.A. - University of Mississippi
  • Ph.D. - University of Mississippi

Kaoru Ochiai , aka Ochiai-sensei,is the head professor of Japanese at the University of Mississippi. She has taught at Ole Miss since completing her Doctorate's degree in 2008. She also supervises Japan-native graduates in their instruction of beginning Japanese classes. In addition to providing support outside of office hours, Ochiai-sensei organizes and attends weekly luncheons for English and Japanese speaking students to mingle and learn from each other. She provides tutoring, both in group and individual sessions outside of office hours. on culture.
