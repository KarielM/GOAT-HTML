Dust Becomes a Mountain

This proverb is a great description of Ochiai-sensei because to everyone that meets her her kindness has a lasting impact. I've had tons of teachers that have made an impact, but to me Ochiai-sensei will always be the GOAT because of her exceeding kindness. She was a demanding teacher, but she went above and beyond for the students she believed capable of meeting her demands. To avoid me falling behind in my degree because of conflicting scheduling, Ochiai-sensei spoke to my Korean professor and the Dean of Modern Languages to make an exception that allowed me to take both classes coupled with one on one tutoring to make up for the one day I had to be in my other class. She wrote my recommendation letter(s) for me when I applied to study abroad, to join the military, and for my first job. She researched Universities and programs that would allow me to travel to Japan. She even gave me her shoes when broke my sandals after a fall so that I wouldn't have to walk back to my dorm barefoot. Since she also provided support to Japanese students studying at the university, she dealt with hundreds of students yearly, but she went out of her way for all the students she believed in.
